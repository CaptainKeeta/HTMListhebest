The Wonderful World of Undertale!


Undertale is a pixel based RPG game developed by Toby Fox from 2013-2015 where nobody has to die.


The Story

You are 'Frisk', a human who has fallen down into the underground, where the monsters that inhabited the earth alongside humans werebanished. You meet a wide variety of characters, and can choose to befreind them, or kill them in typical RPG fashion. Your goal is to escape the underground, by either helping everyone, hindering everyone, or doing a mix of both. It's up to you!


The Gameplay

Undertale is played froma top-down perspective in the overworld, with 'battles' consisting of an old-school first-person view of the enemy.



Attacks come from the enemy in the form of white shapes that you must avoid or take HP damage.



When the Meter reaches 0, you die! Game over man!
